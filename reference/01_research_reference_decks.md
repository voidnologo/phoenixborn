## C. Archetype notes by Phoenixborn (competitive summary)

- **Maeoni Viper** — best tournament record in Ashes; Silver Snake grows on status tokens + Command Strike + bypass (Hypnotize/Molten Gold) for lethal.
- **Odette Diamondcrest** — best all-arounder; Sword of Virtue + Enter the Fray; Hydra combo, control, and burn variants.
- **Brennen Blackcloud** — fastest clock; Spirit Burn sacrifices allies for guaranteed face damage.
- **Aradel Summergaard** — low-variance Natural control; Water Blast is premier removal.
- **Coal Roarkwin** — spends cards not dice (Slash); One Hundred Blades tempo.
- **James Endersight** — ally swarm; tutors cheap 1-life allies (currently topping tournaments).
- **Hope Everthorn** — Illusion swarm/value; Duplicate copies conjurations (currently topping).
- **Jessa Na Ni** — control into burn/mill; Screams of the Departed.
- **Harold Westraven** — deepest archetype pool; Hunter's Mark trades up for free each round.
- **Echo Greystorm** — Time/gravity exhaustion control; Realm Walker + Chaos Gravity + Light Bringer.
- **Rin Northfell** — 10 Natural big-units; Adaptodon + Shattering Fist round-1 value.
- **Namine Hymntide** — Sympathy monster-hunter; Salamander Monk, Guilt Link, Magic Syphon.
- **Xander Heartsblood** — 20-life long-game control; Reincarnate, Earthquake.
- **Lulu Firststone** — aggro/burn; Phoenix Barrage.
- **Koji Wolfcub** — token swarm on a 10-slot battlefield.
- **Dimona Odinstar** — mount aggro into a dragon finisher.
- **Victoria / Noah / Fiona** — Illusion control/disruption.
- **Astrea** — Divine control/burn (Judgment, Beguile).
- **Jericho Reborn** — primarily a co-op (Red Rains) Phoenixborn; off-meta in PvP.
