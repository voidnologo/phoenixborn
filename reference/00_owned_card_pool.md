# Ashes Reborn — OWNED CARD POOL (authoritative, from ashes.live DB)

## Box model
- Every box ships 3x of each standard card (Ready/Action/Reaction/Alteration/Ally).
- Conjurations/Conjured Alterations ship in fixed per-card counts (given below).
- No card is EVER reprinted across boxes, so "cards in box X" = the full owned supply of those cards.
- Deckbuilding rules: 1 Phoenixborn + exactly 30 deck cards, max 3 copies of any one card, up to 10 dice, plus a conjuration pile (conjurations do NOT count toward the 30).

## ERRATA that matters for these decks (current legal text)
- Molten Gold: now PLACES 2 wounds (not direct damage), costs 2 Natural class, off the ban list.
- Ice Trap: cost increased to require a power die.
- Three-Eyed Owl: conjuration count 2; has Peer 1; Memory Drain triggers end of prepare phase (no longer disrupts First Five).
- Emperor Lion: redesigned into a combat conjuration for Divine magic (no longer a Law ready spell).
- Summon Fallen: no longer scales with multiple copies; requires exhausting the ready spell; still makes 3 units for 2 dice.
- Brennen: Life 16; Spirit Burn is Main/Exhaust/1 Ceremonial class -> destroy an ally you control to deal 2 damage to a Phoenixborn.
- Indiglow Creeper conj 1x; Salamander Monk Spirit conj 2x; Meteor is on the chained list.
