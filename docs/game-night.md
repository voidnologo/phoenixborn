# Game Night

**Here are your seven decks.** They're built so you can sleeve up **all seven at the same time** from one collection — everyone grabs one, you play, you swap, you cycle through them all night. No card runs short; see [Sharing the Collection](matrix.md) for the proof.

Each is a solid, proven archetype, tuned to be easy to pick up — no fiddly combos for their own sake — and every one mixes cards from a few different Phoenixborn so you're playing real constructed decks, not the out-of-the-box precons. Want to go further afterward? The [Deck Library](decks.md) has a big menu of extra decks to explore.

<div class="chips"><span class="chip">![Ceremonial](assets/dice/ceremonial.png){.die} ![Natural](assets/dice/natural.png){.die}</span><span class="chip">Aggro / Burn</span><span class="chip">Difficulty ★☆☆</span></div>

The fastest clock in the set. Make cheap bodies and turn them into face damage: Sleeping Widows and Fire Archers become fuel for Spirit Burn and Final Cry, while Molten Gold + Fester clears blockers. Just race.

## 2. Maeoni Viper — "Strike, Scream, Run"

<div class="chips"><span class="chip">![Charm](assets/dice/charm.png){.die} ![Natural](assets/dice/natural.png){.die}</span><span class="chip">Combo</span><span class="chip">Difficulty ★★★</span></div>

**Dice:** ![Charm](assets/dice/charm.png){.die} 5 · ![Natural](assets/dice/natural.png){.die} 5
**Ability — Command Strike:** an unexhausted unit deals its attack to a target unit.

Grow one enormous Silver Snake — every enemy unit that dies (to Frost Bite, Freezing Blast, Ice Trap) adds a token, and its attack equals its tokens. Command Strike snipes units to grow it; then Hypnotize grants Bypass and you swing at the Phoenixborn. Golden Veil protects the snake.

**First Five:** Summon Silver Snake · Hypnotize · Golden Veil · Summon Three-Eyed Owl · Frost Bite

## 5. Namine Hymntide — "Harsh Melody"

<div class="chips"><span class="chip">![Sympathy](assets/dice/sympathy.png){.die} ![Charm](assets/dice/charm.png){.die}</span><span class="chip">Sympathy punish-tokens</span><span class="chip">Difficulty ★★☆</span></div>

**Ability — Calming Melody:** draw a card; you may exhaust a target Phoenixborn.

Flood the board with cheap, recurring bodies — Salamander Monks re-form as Spirits, Squall Stallion grows as you filter — and chip the opponent down. Every hit they land on your Phoenixborn feeds **Sympathy Pain** and **Guilt Link** into free removal, while Crescendo and Shatter Pulse pick off blockers.

## 6. Jericho, Reborn — "Borrowed Time"

<div class="chips"><span class="chip">![Time](assets/dice/time.png){.die} ![Illusion](assets/dice/illusion.png){.die}</span><span class="chip">Time tempo-swarm</span><span class="chip">Difficulty ★★☆</span></div>

**Dice:** ![Time](assets/dice/time.png){.die} 7 · ![Illusion](assets/dice/illusion.png){.die} 3
**Ability — Prepare:** set a card from your draw pile onto the top of your deck.

Get wide fast: Time Hoppers self-replicate and drip status tokens onto your attackers, with Mist Spirits and Swift Messengers adding free bodies. Standard Bearer plus Flash Strike / Outmatch turns a token-loaded attacker into a huge hit, while Figures in the Fog and Stasis simply delete an incoming threat.

**First Five:** Summon Time Hopper · Standard Bearer · Swift Messenger · Accelerate · Summon Mist Spirit

## 7. James Endersight — "The Endless Horde"

Flood the board with disposable tokens — Rising Horde and dying allies feed **Summon Fallen**, which makes three unpreventable-damage Fallen at a time. Immortal Commander gives the whole swarm +1 attack, then Rally the Troops sacrifices spare bodies for a lethal alpha strike. Reclaim Soul and Vengeance keep you topped up.
