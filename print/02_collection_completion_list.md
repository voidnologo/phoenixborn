# Ashes — Completing Your Collection (What You're Missing)

*A shopping list built by cross-referencing your 27 owned Phoenixborn against the complete Ashes release history (all 31 releases in the ashes.live database, spanning the original 1.0 line, Ashes Reborn, and Ashes Ascendancy).*

## The short version

Your collection is remarkably complete. You own **25 of the 28 player-versus-player Phoenixborn** ever released, plus the Ascendancy Starter. What you're missing falls into three buckets:

1. **Three PvP constructed expansions** — the last gap in your competitive collection, and the priority if you want a truly complete constructed pool.
2. **The Ascendancy expansion cycle** — new content built on the two new magic types, only just beginning to release.
3. **The entire Red Rains co-op line** — optional, since your group plays PvP, but it's the only way to get solo/cooperative Ashes.

A note on how the eras fit together: the original **1.0** line (2016–2019) and **Ashes Reborn** (2021+) contain the *same* Phoenixborn — Reborn simply re-released the 1.0 catalog with revised cards (the job your 1.5/2.0 upgrade kit did). So there is no reason to chase the old 1.0 white boxes for play; the Reborn/Ascendancy versions are the tournament-legal ones. **Ashes Ascendancy** (2024+) is the current starting point; when it launched, the Reborn Master Set and all expansions moved to **print-on-demand** (still legal, just no longer on shelves).

---

## 1. Missing PvP Constructed Expansions — *the real gap*

These are the three standard expansion decks you don't own. Each adds a new Phoenixborn and its full card set to your constructed pool. Owning these would make your PvP collection **100% complete** and would let you build the couple of "seven decks from one collection" article decks (and fully optimized versions of others) that currently need cards you lack.

| Expansion | Phoenixborn | Magic focus | Why you'd want it |
|---|---|---|---|
| **The Scholar of Ruin** | Rowan Umberend | Ceremonial / Time (ruin & summoning) | Adds the "Cultist" archetype and staples like Excavate, Discovery, Era's End, Crypt Guardian, Ruin Dweller. |
| **The Ocean's Guard** | Tristan Darkwater | Natural / aquatic | Adds the Prism Tetra swarm engine, Tsunami Shot, Tidal Shift, Wave Crash, Radiant Leviathan — a whole water-tempo package. |
| **The Messenger of Peace** | Orrick Gilstream | Charm / Divine | Adds Ruby Cobra, Sunshield Sentry, Fate Reflection, Recollect, Generosity, Rile the Meek — several widely-used competitive staples. |

*Availability: all three are now print-on-demand through Plaid Hat Games, or available on the secondary market.*

---

## 2. Ashes Ascendancy Expansions — *new content, just starting*

You own the **Ascendancy Starter Set** (Arren Frostpeak + Issa Brightmore, introducing the new **Astral** and **Artifice** magic types). Ascendancy is a planned seven-set cycle; each set pairs the two new magic types with an older dice color and adds two new Phoenixborn plus a co-op Dragonborn boss. As of mid-2026 only the first has released:

| Expansion | Phoenixborn | Status |
|---|---|---|
| **Ascendancy: Natural Magic Expansion** | Maeve Luminvale + Kanna | Released ~Q2 2026 — **currently available** |
| Remaining Ascendancy cycle (~6 more sets) | 2 new Phoenixborn each | Not yet released — buy as they ship |

*Recommendation: pick up the Natural Magic Expansion when convenient, then add future Ascendancy sets on release. Double-check current availability at purchase time, since this line is actively rolling out.*

---

## 3. Red Rains — Solo & Co-op (Optional)

Red Rains is the cooperative/solo mode: you (and optionally a partner) fight an AI-run **Chimera** boss. It's a different experience from your PvP game nights, so treat this as **nice-to-have, not required**. That said, each set also includes a mono-magic preconstructed deck and new "second unique" cards for four existing Phoenixborn, so there's some constructed value too. There are seven sets:

| Red Rains Set | Magic | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| The Corpse of Viros | Ceremonial | The entry point; required to play **The End of Argaia**. |
| The Frostwild Scourge | Natural | Also bundled the first errata wave. |
| The Blight of Neverset | Charm | |
| The Spawn of Shadowreck | Illusion | Also bundled the second errata wave. |
| The Siege of Lordswall | Divine | |
| The Flood of Moon Cove | Sympathy | |
| **The End of Argaia** | Ruin/Rebirth Chimera | The finale of the Red Rains cycle and the last expansion to carry the "Reborn" name; lets you play Jericho, Rowan, Tristan, or Orrick against a new boss. Requires the Master Set + The Corpse of Viros + 10 Time dice. |

*Availability: print-on-demand or secondary market. If you ever want to try co-op, start with **The Corpse of Viros**.*

---

## Priority recommendation

1. **First**, if you want a complete *constructed* collection: **The Scholar of Ruin**, **The Ocean's Guard**, and **The Messenger of Peace**. These are the only PvP decks missing from your binder.
2. **Second**, to stay current with the game's active development: the **Ascendancy: Natural Magic Expansion**, then future Ascendancy sets as they release.
3. **Optional**, only if your group wants solo/co-op play: the **Red Rains** line, starting with **The Corpse of Viros**.
