# Ashes: Rise of the Phoenixborn — Recommended First Five for Every Prebuilt

*A quick-reference for playing each **preconstructed deck** straight out of its box. These are the recommended opening hands for the precons themselves — not the constructed Game Night decks (those have their own Starting Fives in the deck guide). Use this when you want to learn a Phoenixborn by playing its stock deck, or to hand a newcomer a ready-to-go opening.*

**Sources.** The primary First Five for each deck is the official *Suggested First Five* published on that precon's [ashes.live](https://ashes.live) deck page, cross-checked against the community [*Recommended First Fives*](https://wiki.ashes.live/books/rulebook/page/recommended-first-fives) wiki page. Where the two disagree, the ashes.live official list is shown and the wiki's variant is noted with a †. The two **Ascendancy Starter** decks have no official First Five published yet — those entries are labeled as suggestions.
